Poster series, 2011. Text was taken from a series of children's science books (authored by Isaac Asimov). Chunks of paragraphs were removed to give passages an alternate meaning, leaving questions unanswered and vandalizing the original message.

Glitch

Image manipulation, 2011. High resolution images were imported into Photoshop, split into 3 channels (CMY), and exported to RAW format. The RAW files were imported into an audio manipulation program and cut, overlayed with digital effects, and tortured. The files were then spliced together with what remained of the original data in Photoshop.
