projects

Copy Copy Paste

Digital collage / typography experiment. Various magazines and books were cut up, scanned, and pasted back together in Photoshop to convey mixed messages.

Glitch

Image manipulation. High resolution images were imported into Photoshop, split into 3 channels (CMY), and exported to RAW format. The RAW files were imported into an audio manipulation program and cut, overlayed with digital effects, and tortured. The files were then spliced together with what remained of the original data in Photoshop.

Things

Poster series. Text was taken from a series of children's science books (authored by Isaac Asimov). Chunks of paragraphs were removed to give passages an alternate meaning, leaving questions unanswered and vandalizing the original message.
